Our Specialties

Complete Women's Health Services

Obstetrics & Safe Delivery

Expert antenatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, and safe deliveries in fully equipped labour suites.

Laparoscopic Gynaecology

Minimally invasive surgery for fibroids, endometriosis, ovarian cysts, and hysterectomy.

Fertility & IVF

Fertility workup, IUI, IVF/ICSI, egg freezing, and reproductive endocrinology.

Gynaecological Oncology

Cancer screening, colposcopy, and treatment for cervical, ovarian, and uterine malignancies.

Urogynaecology

Treatment of pelvic floor disorders, urinary incontinence, and pelvic organ prolapse.

Menopause Clinic

Hormone therapy, bone density management, and holistic menopause care.

Prenatal Diagnostics

NT scan, anomaly scan, fetal echo, NIPT, and amniocentesis.

Well-Woman Clinic

Annual health checkups, PAP smear, breast examination, and contraceptive counseling.

NICU Liaison

Seamless coordination with the NICU for early deliveries and neonatal complications.
